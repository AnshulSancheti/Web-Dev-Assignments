Cheese-Maggi

Ingredients

  • One Packet Of Maggi
  • Peri-Peri
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Add water to pan
  2. Put Maggi in pan
  3. boil
  4. Add Maggi Masala
  5. Add Peri-Peri
  6. mix
  7. add Cream
  8. Serve in a bowl
  9. Place the slice of cheese on top
  10. eat
